Stores Assistant
Job Overview:

Understanding the Purchase order to cash process, should have good knowledge of inventory /stock keeping. Working in tandem with commercial & service team for product and spare parts delivery.

Reports To

The Stores Assistant will report to the Commercial Head.

Duties & Responsibilities:
  • To update all the stocks on an online basis so that there is no mismatch between entry and delivery of products.
  • To dispatch and co-ordinate with logistics team(outsourced) and dispatch as per plan.
  • To create, maintain and update Bin cards whenever applicable.
  • To classify the stock according to fast moving and slow moving.
  • To create, maintain and update stock register on an on-going basis.
  • To update stock register with the buying price.
  • To create, maintain and update Inward and dispatch register.
  • To create, maintain and update transporter register with rates.
Qualifications:
  • Education level: ITI or should have completed two Year Pre-University course.
  • Experience: Hands-on experience of one year in stock keeping.
  • Specific skills: Knowledge of basic English, Kannada & any other south Indian languages would be advantage.
  • Personal characteristics: Patience, Perseverance & Hardworking.
  • Physical abilities: Should be ready to travel for deliveries.

Sales Engineer
Job Overview:

Understanding of process knowledge to offer solutions to customers is the key role of technical sales. It is very important to have a basic knowledge of Water and Wastewater Treatment (which will be trained by our company).

Reports To

The Sales Engineer will report to the Sales Head.

Duties & Responsibilities:
  • To make regular planning of sales visits to prospective customers.
  • To generate enquiries for various products and services – Water and Wastewater Treatment equipment and allied products.
  • To follow up the given proposals with customer/proposal team.
  • To prepare sales report daily.
  • To prepare Target Vs Actual plan daily for sales.
Qualifications:
  • Education level: Candidate to have Diploma in Chemical/Mechanical or Bachelor of Engineering with Chemical or Mechanical.
  • Experience: Hands-on experience of one year in proposal making in any engineering firm.
  • Specific skills: Language Proficiency, fluent English and Kannada (writing/speaking) and knowing any of the South Indian and Hindi languages is an added advantage.
  • Personal characteristics: Go getter attitude, Perseverance & Hardworking
  • Physical abilities: Should be ready to travel extensively for training and sales calls.
