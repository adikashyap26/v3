About Vijetha

VIJETHA, a vibrant and innovative company, emerged in 2008 and is headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka. With over 15+ years of profound technical expertise, our focus lies in designing, streamlining processes, executing commissioning tasks, and adeptly troubleshooting issues related to water and wastewater treatments. At Vijetha, we pride ourselves on offering comprehensive solutions catering to diverse water, sewage, and effluent treatment needs.

Our endeavor is to not just address technical intricacies but to provide solutions with a professional touch, ensuring seamless operations for our valued clients across industries.
