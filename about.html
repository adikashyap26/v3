Know Us Better

In 2008, we started this journey of offering single window solutions of all Water and Waste Water Treatment being a Thermax Channel Associate.VIJETHA has continued to Innovate and Evolve , becoming one of the preferred solution provider to all the Problems related to Water and Waste Water. Today , we service more than 50 + customers than ever before.We are already in the verge of completing decade in this industry. Having a well trained and experienced team on board.We have worked with different Process Industries like Steel, Power, Pharmaceutical, Chemical, Automobile, Food & Beverages, Textile, Distilleries etc.

Roopesh BS is a seasoned professional in the field of wastewater treatment, boasting an impressive career spanning over 25 years.
