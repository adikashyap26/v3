CONDENSERS & DRY COOLERS

Condensers and drycoolers are important components in refrigeration systems, ensuring that the heat generated is efficiently discharged to the ambient air. Our condensers, gas coolers and drycoolers thus ensure efficient and safe cooling operation. Are you looking for a unit with a better CO2 balance, quieter operation, higher power density or a smaller footprint? We are sure to have the optimal solution for you.

CUSTOMIZED CONDENSERS AND RECOOLERS

Our customized condensers and drycoolers offer the ideal solution for applications that have special requirements. V-SHAPE COOLER (V-COOLER)

Our V-coolers are available in various sizes and offer an optimal solution for diverse applications ranging from warehouses and data centers to indoor climate control and industrial cooling. The condensers, gas coolers and recirculating chillers are space-saving due to their narrow V-shape, yet still exhibit high thermal performance. Other advantages of our V-coolers include lower noise levels and more energy-efficient operation. Thanks to the larger surface area, the V-shaped condensers and recoolers can transfer heat more effectively.
